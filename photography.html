PHOTOGRAPHY

The page you are watching shows my photography works. These are mainly photos taken during my travels, as well as some everyday photos covering people, landscapes, animals, food, night scenes, objects, experimentation with different photo styles and a variety of lighting and tone. Photography is a mean of artistic language expression. There is no significant difference between photography and other forms of artistic communication (a carrier of one or more ideas, emotions, cultures, commercial forms, etc.).

The meaning of photography is to record history with a camera, to freeze history in a moment and make the moment eternal. Photography has been given the mission of recording from the moment it appeared. In the journey of shooting, the sea, the earth, the stars, the changing landscapes, the people I meet, the animals and plants that I did not know, everything that interests me. I respect the power of nature. Exploring and listening to the mysterious inner world and recording them.
